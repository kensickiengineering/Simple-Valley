Why We're Doing This

The Simple Valley Story & Philosophy

A Simple Frustration

Just a few years ago, I reached a point where food started to feel more complicated than it needed to be. Surrounded by tons of conflicting information online, I was tired of constantly experimenting with new products, new rules, and new fixes. Nothing really stuck. What I wanted wasn’t another trend, it was something simple and dependable that could fit into my everyday routine without much thought.


That search led me back to real, traditional foods and a more stripped-down way of eating. Less noise. Fewer ingredients. More consistency. I didn’t set out to start a company, I was just trying to build better habits and feel more grounded in my day-to-day life.

Building a Daily Routine

Over time, simplicity became the foundation. I focused on foods that felt functional, familiar, and easy to return to, especially when life got busy. One ingredient that kept showing up in my routine was beef liver. Not because it was trendy or exciting, but because it was traditional, nutrient-rich, and something humans have relied on for generations.


The problem was obvious: it wasn’t practical for everyday life. Eating liver on its own wasn’t appealing, and most of the products I found were either overly processed, overpriced, or filled with ingredients that didn’t align with the simple approach I was looking for. I simply wanted the benefits of traditional nutrition in a format that worked quietly in the background of a modern life.

Why Simple Valley Exists

Simple Valley was created to make that kind of food easier to reach for. Not as a shortcut, but as a tool—something that supports movement, work, training, and everyday life without overthinking it. Our bars are built with whole food ingredients, animal-based protein, and no unnecessary fillers, designed to be simple enough to enjoy daily.


We stripped away the noise to get back to what works: nutrient-dense ingredients that work with your body, not against it. With 20 grams of protein and a clean label, Simple Valley offers the nourishment of a traditional diet in the convenience of a modern wrapper. No gimmicks, just honest fuel to keep you going, regardless of what life throws your way. Keep it Simple.
